Link to [Envy](docs/sidebar1/Objective/bad%20traits/Envy.md): 
- say Tabaraka Lah or macha'alah when witnessing a blessing. 
- Help people who are more blessed, be humble with them, praise them, and feel joy for them (if you can't help them physically, pray for them).
- Don't look greedily at what other people possess so you don't desire what they have (status or wealth). You can do this by unfollowing someone wealthy on social media.
